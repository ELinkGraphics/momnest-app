## 3. DMCA Takedown Procedure

### 3.1 Filing a Copyright Complaint
If you believe your copyrighted work has been posted on MomsNest without authorization, submit a written notice containing:

1. **Identification of the copyrighted work** — description of the original work being infringed
2. **Identification of the infringing material** — URL, post ID, or screenshot of the content on MomsNest
3. **Your contact information** — full name, email address, phone number, mailing address
4. **Good faith statement** — "I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law."
5. **Accuracy statement** — "The information in this notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, I am the owner, or authorized to act on behalf of the owner, of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed."
6. **Your signature** — physical or electronic signature

### 3.2 Where to Send Notices
Send copyright complaints to:
- **Email:** legal@momsnest.com
- **Subject:** "DMCA Takedown Request — [Brief Description]"

### 3.3 Our Response Process
Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice:
1. **Acknowledgment** — We will acknowledge receipt within **48 hours**
2. **Review** — We will review the notice for completeness and validity
3. **Action** — If valid, we will remove or disable access to the alleged infringing material **within 72 hours**
4. **Notification** — We will notify the user who posted the content of the takedown
5. **Record** — We will maintain records of all DMCA notices received

## 4. Counter-Notification

If your content was removed due to a DMCA notice and you believe it was a mistake or misidentification, you may file a counter-notification containing:

1. **Identification of the removed material** — description and former location on MomsNest
2. **Good faith statement** — "I swear, under penalty of perjury, that I have a good faith belief that the material was removed or disabled as a result of a mistake or misidentification."
3. **Your contact information** — full name, email address, phone number
4. **Consent to jurisdiction** — consent to the jurisdiction of the federal court in your district
5. **Your signature** — physical or electronic signature

Send counter-notifications to: **legal@momsnest.com**  
Subject: "DMCA Counter-Notification — [Brief Description]"

### Counter-Notification Process
1. We will forward the counter-notification to the original complainant
2. The complainant has **10 business days** to file a court action
3. If no court action is filed, the content may be restored within **14 business days**

## 5. Repeat Infringer Policy

MomsNest maintains a **repeat infringer policy.** Users who are the subject of repeated valid DMCA complaints may face:

| Offense | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **First offense** | Content removed + warning |
| **Second offense** | Content removed + 7-day suspension |
| **Third offense** | Content removed + 30-day suspension |
| **Fourth offense** | Permanent account termination |

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT — READ BEFORE USING THE SOS FEATURE

By using the SOS/Safety features of MomsNest, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

### 1. MomsNest Is NOT an Emergency Service
- MomsNest is a **community-based support tool**, NOT a replacement for professional emergency services (police, ambulance, fire department)
- **Always call your local emergency number (e.g., 911, 911, 991) FIRST** in any life-threatening situation
- MomsNest does not guarantee response times, helper availability, or emergency outcomes

### 2. Community Helpers Are Volunteers
- Helpers who respond to SOS alerts are **community volunteers**, not employees, agents, or contractors of MomsNest
- MomsNest does **NOT verify** the professional qualifications, certifications, or background of helpers
- Helpers are under **no obligation** to respond to any alert
- MomsNest does **NOT guarantee** the quality, appropriateness, or effectiveness of any assistance provided by helpers

### 3. Location Sharing Consent
- When you create an SOS alert, your **precise GPS location** may be shared with:
  - Nearby MomsNest users (within a configured radius)
  - Your designated emergency contacts
  - Helpers who accept your request
- You can **stop sharing your location** at any time by resolving the alert
- Location data is stored securely and used **only** for the purpose of the SOS alert

### 4. Limitation of Liability
- MomsNest accepts **NO LIABILITY** for:
  - Any physical harm, injury, or death resulting from use of the SOS feature
  - Delayed, incomplete, or unsuccessful emergency response
  - Actions, inactions, or misconduct of community helpers
  - Failure of the app, network, or GPS to function as expected
  - Inaccurate location data or failed notifications
  - Consequences of sharing your location with other users

### 5. False Alert Policy
- Filing **false or frivolous SOS alerts** is strictly prohibited
- False alerts waste community resources and erode trust
- Consequences include **immediate account suspension** and potential **legal action**

### 6. Your Responsibilities
By using the SOS feature, you agree to:
- ✅ Contact professional emergency services FIRST for life-threatening situations
- ✅ Provide accurate and truthful information in your alerts
- ✅ Use the feature only for genuine emergency or safety concerns
- ✅ Respect the privacy and safety of helpers who respond
- ✅ Accept that MomsNest is a supplementary tool, not a primary emergency service

### 7. Mental Health Emergencies
For mental health crises, including thoughts of self-harm or suicide:
- **Contact your local mental health helpline immediately**
- MomsNest is a peer support community, **NOT a crisis intervention service**
- Our community can provide emotional support, but professional help should always be your first step
