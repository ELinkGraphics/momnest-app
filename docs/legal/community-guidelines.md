# Community Guidelines

**MomsNest — Community Guidelines**  
**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026  

---

## Welcome to MomsNest 💜

MomsNest is a safe, supportive space built by mothers, for mothers. These Community Guidelines help us maintain a welcoming environment where every mom feels respected, heard, and empowered.

**By using MomsNest, you agree to follow these guidelines.** Violations may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination.

---

## 🫶 Our Core Values

1. **Respect** — Treat every member with kindness and empathy
2. **Safety** — Protect yourself and others from harm
3. **Honesty** — Share genuine experiences and truthful information
4. **Support** — Lift each other up, especially during tough times
5. **Privacy** — Protect the personal information of all members

---

## ✅ DO — Encouraged Behavior

### Be Supportive & Kind
- Share encouragement and positive experiences
- Offer advice when asked, with compassion and understanding
- Recognize that every mother's journey is different
- Use inclusive language that welcomes all backgrounds

### Share Authentic Content
- Post genuine experiences, questions, and stories
- Credit sources when sharing articles or research
- Clearly distinguish personal experience from professional advice
- Label sponsored content or paid partnerships

### Build Community
- Participate in circles and discussions constructively
- Organize events that bring mothers together
- Mentor new or struggling mothers
- Report content that violates these guidelines

### Marketplace Best Practices
- Provide accurate product descriptions and images
- Price items fairly and transparently
- Communicate with buyers/sellers promptly
- Honor refund and return agreements

---

## ❌ DO NOT — Prohibited Behavior

### Harassment & Bullying
- **No personal attacks**, insults, or name-calling directed at other users
- **No mom-shaming** — parenting choices (breastfeeding vs. formula, co-sleeping, screen time, etc.) are personal decisions and should not be criticized
- **No body-shaming** of any kind (pregnancy weight, postpartum body changes)
- **No targeting** individuals with repeated unwanted messages or comments
- **No doxxing** — sharing another person's private information without consent

### Hate Speech & Discrimination
- **No content promoting** hate based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality
- **No slurs or derogatory language** targeting any group
- **No trolling** designed to provoke or upset community members

### Harmful Content
- **No dangerous medical advice** presented as fact (e.g., "don't vaccinate" without medical evidence)
- **No promotion of eating disorders**, self-harm, or suicidal content
- **No graphic violence** or disturbing imagery
- **No content promoting** illegal activities, substances, or dangerous challenges

### Misinformation
- **No fake health claims** (unproven cures, dangerous remedies for children)
- **No conspiracy theories** targeting vulnerable populations
- **No impersonation** of medical professionals or experts
- Label opinions clearly as opinions, not facts

### Spam & Commercial Abuse
- **No unsolicited advertising** or self-promotion in comments
- **No fake accounts** or bot activity
- **No pyramid schemes**, MLM recruitment, or scam promotions
- **No review manipulation** (fake reviews, paid reviews without disclosure)

### Explicit Content
- **No sexually explicit** content or nudity (breastfeeding photos are permitted and encouraged)
- **No pornographic material** of any kind
- **No content depicting** child abuse or exploitation

### Safety Violations
- **No false SOS alerts** or misuse of the emergency feature
- **No predatory behavior** toward other users or their children
- **No solicitation** of personal meetings with minors

---

## 📍 Special Guidelines by Feature

### Social Feed
- Keep posts relevant to motherhood, parenting, family, or personal growth
- Use content warnings for sensitive topics (loss, trauma, medical issues)
- Tag posts appropriately with relevant categories

### Community Circles
- Follow circle-specific guidelines set by the circle administrator
- Respect the circle's privacy level (public vs. private discussions)
- Contribute meaningfully — avoid low-effort or off-topic posts

### Ask Q&A
- **Anonymous asking is encouraged** for sensitive topics — do not try to identify anonymous posters
- Clearly distinguish professional advice from personal experience
- Respect verified experts and their professional boundaries
- Do not demand free consultations from experts

### Marketplace
- Do not list recalled, unsafe, or age-inappropriate products
- Accurately describe product condition (new, used, refurbished)
- Do not engage in price gouging during emergencies or shortages
- Complete transactions within the platform

### SOS/Safety
- Use the SOS feature only for genuine emergencies
- If you accept a helper request, respond promptly and honestly about your arrival
- Never share another user's SOS location outside the platform
- Report any suspicious behavior from helpers immediately

### Live Streaming
- No streaming content that would be inappropriate in front of children
- Moderate your live chat — report abusive chatters
- No streaming of other people's copyrighted content

## 🚨 Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report
1. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** on any post, comment, or profile
2. Select **"Report"**
3. Choose the violation category
4. Add any additional context
5. Submit — our team will review within **24–48 hours**

### Enforcement Actions

| Level | Action | Duration | Trigger |
|-------|--------|----------|---------|
| **1 — Warning** | Private warning message | N/A | First minor violation |
| **2 — Content Removal** | Offending content deleted | Permanent | Confirmed violation |
| **3 — Temporary Suspension** | Account suspended | 3–30 days | Repeated or serious violations |
| **4 — Permanent Ban** | Account permanently disabled | Permanent | Severe violations, safety threats |
| **5 — Legal Action** | Reported to law enforcement | Permanent | Illegal activity, child endangerment |

### Appeal Process
If you believe your content was removed or account suspended unfairly:
1. Email **appeals@momsnest.com** with your username and details
2. Include any context or evidence supporting your appeal
3. Appeals are reviewed within **7 business days**
4. The appeal decision is final

## 🤝 A Note on Mental Health

Motherhood can be overwhelming. If you or someone you know is struggling:
- **Share in the community** — you are not alone
- **Use anonymous posting** for sensitive topics
- **Reach out to professionals** — our expert Q&A connects you with verified counselors

> **If you are in immediate danger or having thoughts of self-harm, please contact your local emergency services or a mental health helpline immediately. MomsNest is a support community, not a crisis service.**
