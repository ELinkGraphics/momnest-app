# Helper Agreement

**MomsNest — SOS Community Helper Agreement**  
**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026  

---

## 1. Introduction

This Helper Agreement ("Agreement") governs your participation as a Community Helper in the MomsNest SOS/Safety module. By registering as a helper or responding to any SOS alert, you agree to be bound by this Agreement.

> **⚠️ IMPORTANT:** You are volunteering as a community support helper, NOT acting as a professional emergency responder. Always advise users in distress to contact professional emergency services.

---

## 2. Role Definition

### 2.1 What a Community Helper IS
- A **community volunteer** who chooses to assist other mothers during difficult situations
- A person willing to provide **emotional support, guidance, or physical presence** when nearby
- A member of the MomsNest community who has opted into the helper network

### 2.2 What a Community Helper IS NOT
- ❌ An employee, agent, or contractor of MomsNest
- ❌ A professional emergency responder (paramedic, firefighter, police)
- ❌ A certified counselor or therapist (unless separately qualified)
- ❌ A medical professional (unless separately qualified)
- ❌ Obligated to respond to any alert

---

## 3. Eligibility

To register as a Community Helper, you must:
- Be a registered MomsNest user for at least **30 days**
- Be at least **18 years of age**
- Have a verified email address
- Complete the helper profile with skills, availability, and contact info
- Accept this Helper Agreement and the SOS Safety Disclaimer
- Have no history of abuse reports or Terms violations

---

## 4. Helper Profile

### 4.1 Required Information
| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Skills** | Areas you can help with (emotional support, transport, childcare, first aid, etc.) |
| **Availability** | Days and hours you are typically available |
| **Location** | Approximate area for proximity matching |
| **Phone** | Contact number for urgent situations |

### 4.2 Optional Information
| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Certifications** | First aid, CPR, nursing, counseling certifications |
| **Languages** | Languages spoken |
| **Transportation** | Whether you have a vehicle |
| **Bio** | Brief description of yourself and motivation |

### 4.3 Profile Accuracy
- All profile information must be accurate and up-to-date
- Misrepresenting your skills or certifications is a violation of this Agreement
- Update your availability status when it changes

---

## 5. Responding to Alerts

### 5.1 Alert Flow
```
SOS Alert Created → Nearby Helpers Notified → You Choose to Accept/Decline
     ↓ (if accepted)
Review Alert Details → Navigate to Location → Provide Assistance → Mark Complete
     ↓ (throughout)
Real-time Chat with Alerter → Location Sharing
```

### 5.2 Your Obligations When Accepting
By accepting an alert, you agree to:
- ✅ **Arrive promptly** at the indicated location (or communicate delays)
- ✅ **Provide your current location** so the alerter can track your arrival
- ✅ **Communicate** via the SOS messaging feature
- ✅ **Prioritize the safety** of the person in distress
- ✅ **Encourage contacting** professional emergency services when appropriate
- ✅ **Be honest** about your estimated arrival time
- ✅ **Mark completion** when the situation is resolved or you can no longer assist

### 5.3 Your Right to Decline
- You are **never obligated** to accept any alert
- You may decline without penalty or explanation
- If you accept but become unavailable, notify the alerter immediately via chat
- Repeated acceptance-then-abandonment may affect your helper rating

---

## 6. Standards of Conduct

### 6.1 Required Behavior
| Standard | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Respectful communication** | Treat alerters with empathy, patience, and kindness |
| **Privacy protection** | Never share an alerter's location, identity, or situation outside the platform |
| **Honest representation** | Do not claim professional skills you do not possess |
| **Non-judgmental support** | Provide help without judgment regardless of the situation |
| **Boundary respect** | Respect the alerter's physical and emotional boundaries |
| **Safety first** | Never put yourself in danger to assist others |

### 6.2 Prohibited Behavior
| Prohibition | Consequence |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Exploiting vulnerable users** (financially, emotionally, physically) | Immediate permanent ban + law enforcement |
| **Sharing alerter's private information** (location, identity, situation) | Permanent ban |
| **Soliciting personal relationships** from SOS interactions | Suspension |
| **Providing medical advice** without proper qualifications | Warning → suspension |
| **Accepting alerts with no intention to help** | Warning → removal from helper network |
| **Using SOS data for stalking or harassment** | Permanent ban + law enforcement |
| **Demanding payment or compensation** from alerters | Suspension |

## 7. Ratings & Accountability

### 7.1 Helper Ratings
- Alerters may rate helpers after each completed interaction (1–5 stars)
- Ratings are displayed on your helper profile
- Low ratings (consistently below 3 stars) may trigger a review

### 7.2 Helper Stats
| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Response rate** | % of accepted vs. total notifications |
| **Average rating** | Mean star rating from all interactions |
| **Completion rate** | % of accepted alerts completed vs. abandoned |
| **Total assists** | Lifetime count of completed assists |
| **Helper streak** | Consecutive days with completed assists |

### 7.3 Badges & Recognition
| Badge | Requirement |
|-------|------------|
| **First Responder** | Complete first SOS assist |
| **Trusted Helper** | 10+ assists with 4+ star rating |
| **Community Guardian** | 50+ assists with 4.5+ star rating |
| **Helper of the Month** | Top-rated helper in a calendar month |

## 9. Confidentiality

### 9.1 Information You May Access
During SOS interactions, you may receive:
- Alerter's approximate or exact location
- Nature of the emergency
- Personal details shared in SOS messages
- Alerter's name and profile information

### 9.2 Confidentiality Obligations
You agree to:
- **Never share** alerter information outside the MomsNest SOS context
- **Never screenshot** or record SOS conversations without consent
- **Never use** alerter information for any purpose other than providing assistance
- **Delete** any personal notes or information after the alert is resolved
- **Report** any breaches of confidentiality you become aware of

## 10. Removal from Helper Network

### 10.1 Grounds for Removal
| Reason | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Consistent low ratings (< 2.5 average) | Warning → removal |
| Privacy breach (sharing alerter data) | Immediate removal |
| Inappropriate behavior toward alerters | Investigation → removal |
| Accepting alerts but not showing up (3+ times) | Warning → removal |
| Violation of Community Guidelines | Per violation severity |
| User report confirmed | Investigation → action |

### 10.2 Voluntary Withdrawal
- You may leave the helper network at any time through your profile settings
- This does not affect your regular MomsNest account
- You can re-apply after 30 days if you change your mind
