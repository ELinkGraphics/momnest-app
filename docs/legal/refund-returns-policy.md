# Refund & Returns Policy

**MomsNest — Marketplace Refund & Returns Policy**  
**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026  

---

## 1. Overview

This Refund & Returns Policy governs all marketplace transactions on MomsNest. MomsNest acts as a platform connecting buyers and sellers — we are not a party to transactions, but we provide tools and guidelines to ensure fair outcomes.

---

## 2. Refund Eligibility

### 2.1 Eligible for Refund
| Reason | Refund Type | Window |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **Item not received** | Full refund | 14 days after estimated delivery |
| **Item significantly not as described** | Full refund (item returned) | 14 days after delivery |
| **Wrong item received** | Full refund (item returned) | 14 days after delivery |
| **Item arrived damaged** | Full or partial refund | 7 days after delivery |
| **Order cancelled before shipping** | Full refund | Before seller ships |
| **Seller unable to fulfill** | Full refund | Any time before delivery |

### 2.2 NOT Eligible for Refund
| Reason | Policy |
|--------|--------|
| **Buyer's remorse** (changed mind) | Not eligible unless seller agrees |
| **Item used or altered** after delivery | Not eligible |
| **Minor color/size differences** within listed specs | Not eligible |
| **More than 14 days** since delivery | Not eligible (exception: undiscovered defects) |
| **Personalized/custom items** | Not eligible unless defective |
| **Digital goods** | Not eligible after download/access |
| **Consumable/perishable items** | Not eligible after use/expiry |

---

## 3. Refund Process

### 3.1 Step-by-Step
```
Step 1: Buyer contacts seller via Shop Messages (attempt resolution)
    ↓ (if unresolved after 48 hours)
Step 2: Buyer files a Dispute through the order page
    ↓
Step 3: Seller responds within 48 hours with resolution offer
    ↓ (if buyer rejects or seller doesn't respond)
Step 4: MomsNest mediates and reviews evidence
    ↓
Step 5: Final decision issued within 7 business days
    ↓
Step 6: Refund processed (if approved)
```

### 3.2 Required Evidence
| Party | Evidence |
|-------|---------|
| **Buyer** | Photos of item received, screenshots of listing, shipping label, messages with seller |
| **Seller** | Proof of shipping, tracking number, photos of item before shipping, listing screenshots |

### 3.3 Mediation
If buyer and seller cannot reach an agreement:
- MomsNest staff will review all evidence from both parties
- Decision is based on evidence quality, listing accuracy, and communication history
- MomsNest's mediation decision is **final and binding**

---

## 4. Return Process

### 4.1 Return Conditions
- Items must be returned in their **original condition** (unused, unwashed, with tags if applicable)
- Items must be returned within **7 days** of refund approval
- Buyer must provide proof of return shipping (tracking number)

### 4.2 Return Shipping Costs
| Scenario | Who Pays |
|----------|----------|
| Item not as described | **Seller** pays return shipping |
| Wrong item sent | **Seller** pays return shipping |
| Item damaged in transit | **Seller** pays (claim with carrier) |
| Buyer changed mind (if seller agrees) | **Buyer** pays return shipping |

### 4.3 Return Address
- Seller provides a return address upon refund approval
- If seller does not provide an address within 72 hours, a full refund is issued without return

---

## 5. Refund Methods

### 5.1 Refund Destination
| Original Payment | Refund Method | Timeline |
|-----------------|---------------|----------|
| **Coin wallet** | Coins returned to buyer's wallet | 1–3 business days |
| **Payment gateway** (future) | Refund to original payment method | 5–10 business days |

### 5.2 Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be offered when:
- Item has minor defects but is still usable
- Part of a multi-item order is affected
- Buyer and seller negotiate a partial resolution
- Return shipping cost is split

---

## 6. Seller Refund Obligations

- Sellers must respond to refund requests within **48 hours**
- Refund amounts are deducted from the seller's coin wallet balance
- If the seller's balance is insufficient, future earnings are held until repaid
- Sellers with excessive refund rates (>15% of orders) may face listing restrictions

---

## 7. Disputes

### 7.1 Filing a Dispute
1. Go to **My Orders** → Select the order
2. Tap **"File Dispute"**
3. Select the dispute reason
4. Upload supporting evidence (photos, screenshots)
5. Describe the issue in detail
6. Submit — the seller has **48 hours** to respond

### 7.2 Dispute Timeline
| Stage | Timeline |
|-------|----------|
| Seller response window | 48 hours |
| Buyer counter-response | 48 hours |
| MomsNest mediation review | 3–7 business days |
| Refund processing (if approved) | 1–3 business days |
| **Total maximum resolution time** | **14 business days** |

### 7.3 Dispute Outcomes
| Outcome | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Full refund to buyer** | Seller liable; full amount returned |
| **Partial refund** | Negotiated settlement |
| **No refund** | Buyer's claim not supported by evidence |
| **Dispute withdrawn** | Buyer cancels dispute |

---

## 8. Flash Sales & Group Buys

### 8.1 Flash Sale Refunds
- Flash sale purchases are subject to the same refund policy
- The refund amount is the **discounted price paid**, not the original price

### 8.2 Group Buy Refunds
- Individual participants may request refunds before the group buy closes
- If a group buy does not reach its minimum participants, all participants receive automatic refunds
- After the group buy closes and orders are confirmed, standard refund policy applies

---

## 9. Coin Purchases

### 9.1 Coin Top-Ups
- Coin purchases are **final and non-refundable**
- Exceptions may be made for unauthorized purchases (contact support)

### 9.2 Premium Content Unlocks
- Coins spent on unlocking premium content are **non-refundable**
- If the content is removed by the creator, spent coins are **not returned**
