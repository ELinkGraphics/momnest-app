# Acceptable Use Policy

**MomsNest — Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)**  
**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026  

---

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the permitted and prohibited uses of the MomsNest platform. This policy applies to all users, including individuals, sellers, circle administrators, helpers, and experts.

Violation of this AUP may result in content removal, account suspension, or termination as described in our Terms of Service.

---

## 2. Acceptable Uses

MomsNest may be used for the following purposes:

### Personal & Social
- Sharing personal motherhood experiences, stories, and photos
- Connecting with other mothers through circles and messaging
- Seeking and providing community support and advice
- Creating and consuming stories, posts, and video content
- Following and engaging with other community members

### Community & Circles
- Creating and managing community circles on parenting-related topics
- Organizing circle events (virtual meetups, workshops, playdates)
- Sharing educational resources and downloadable content
- Providing community services through circle service listings

### Marketplace
- Listing and selling baby products, maternity items, and related goods
- Purchasing products from verified community sellers
- Writing honest reviews of purchased products
- Communicating with sellers about products and orders

### Expert Services
- Providing verified expert advice in areas of professional competence
- Answering community questions with accurate, helpful information
- Building a professional profile with legitimate credentials

### Safety & Emergency
- Creating legitimate SOS alerts for genuine emergency situations
- Volunteering as a community helper for emergency response
- Managing emergency contacts for SOS notification
- Reporting abuse or safety concerns through proper channels

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

The following activities are strictly prohibited on MomsNest:

### 3.1 Illegal Activities
- Using the platform to facilitate any illegal activity
- Drug trafficking or promoting controlled substance sales
- Money laundering or financial fraud
- Human trafficking or exploitation
- Distribution of child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Stalking or criminal harassment

### 3.2 System Abuse
- Attempting to gain unauthorized access to accounts, systems, or data
- Exploiting vulnerabilities in the platform (without responsible disclosure)
- Creating multiple accounts to circumvent bans or restrictions
- Using automated bots, scrapers, or crawlers without authorization
- Deliberately disrupting the Service through denial-of-service attacks
- Attempting to reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble the application
- Circumventing security measures, rate limits, or access controls
- Injecting malicious code, scripts, or links

### 3.3 Data Abuse
- Harvesting, scraping, or collecting user data without consent
- Sharing other users' personal information (doxxing)
- Using the platform to phish for credentials or personal data
- Downloading and republishing user content without permission
- Accessing data beyond your authorized scope (violating RLS)

### 3.4 Commercial Abuse
- Listing prohibited items (weapons, drugs, stolen goods, counterfeits)
- Running pyramid schemes, Ponzi schemes, or multi-level marketing
- Publishing fake reviews or engaging in review manipulation
- Fraudulent marketplace transactions (non-delivery, bait-and-switch)
- Price manipulation (artificial scarcity, price gouging)
- Shill bidding on group buys
- Selling unsafe, recalled, or expired products
- Evading marketplace fees through off-platform transactions

### 3.5 Content Abuse
- Posting spam, unsolicited advertising, or irrelevant promotional content
- Impersonating other users, experts, medical professionals, or MomsNest staff
- Creating misleading or fraudulent expert profiles
- Publishing deliberately false information about health, safety, or childcare
- Posting sexually explicit content (breastfeeding and birth content is permitted)
- Posting content that promotes violence, self-harm, or dangerous behavior
- Creating deepfakes or AI-generated misleading content

### 3.6 Coin & Financial Abuse
- Creating fake accounts to earn sign-up bonus coins
- Automating engagement to artificially earn coins
- Exploiting coin transaction bugs or system errors
- Laundering money through the coin purchase/withdrawal system
- Manipulating group buy or tipping systems for profit

### 3.7 SOS/Safety Abuse
- Filing false emergency alerts (including testing SOS in production)
- Using SOS data to stalk, harass, or locate users
- Sharing another user's emergency location outside the platform
- Impersonating emergency responders or professional helpers
- Accepting help requests with no intention to assist

## 5. Monitoring & Enforcement

### 5.1 Monitoring
MomsNest reserves the right to monitor platform usage for compliance with this AUP. Monitoring may include:
- Automated spam and abuse detection
- Manual review of reported content
- System log analysis for suspicious activity
- Marketplace listing quality checks

### 5.2 Violation Response
| Severity | Examples | Action |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **Minor** | Accidental guidelines breach, mild spam | Warning + education |
| **Moderate** | Repeated minor violations, misleading content | 7-day suspension |
| **Severe** | Harassment, fraud, fake SOS alerts | 30-day to permanent ban |
| **Critical** | Illegal activity, CSAM, criminal threats | Immediate ban + law enforcement |

### 5.3 User Cooperation
Users agree to cooperate with MomsNest investigations into potential AUP violations. Refusal to cooperate may be considered a violation in itself.

---

## 6. Reporting Violations

If you encounter a violation of this AUP:
1. Use the in-app **Report** button on the offending content or profile
2. Email **abuse@momsnest.com** for complex or urgent cases
3. For criminal activity, also contact your local law enforcement

We take all reports seriously and will respond within **24–48 hours**.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this AUP at any time. Material changes will be notified via the app. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
