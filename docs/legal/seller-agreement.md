# Seller Agreement

**MomsNest — Marketplace Seller Agreement**  
**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026  

---

## 1. Introduction

This Seller Agreement ("Agreement") governs your participation as a seller on the MomsNest Marketplace. By creating a seller profile or listing any product on MomsNest, you agree to be bound by this Agreement, as well as our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Community Guidelines.

---

## 2. Eligibility

To become a seller on MomsNest, you must:
- Be a registered MomsNest user in good standing
- Be at least **18 years of age**
- Provide accurate seller information (business name, email, location)
- Comply with all applicable local, national, and international laws regarding the sale of goods
- Accept this Seller Agreement in its entirety

---

## 3. Seller Profile & Verification

### 3.1 Profile Setup
To list products, you must create a seller profile with:
- **Required:** Business/display name, email address, location
- **Optional:** Phone number, business description, business license, tax ID, business registration number

### 3.2 Verification Process
| Level | Requirements | Benefits |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Basic Seller** | Email + location | Can list products |
| **Verified Seller** | Business license + Tax ID | Verified badge, higher trust ranking |
| **Premium Seller** | Full documentation + review history | Featured placement, priority support |

### 3.3 Verification Standards
- Verification is conducted by MomsNest staff and may take **3–7 business days**
- MomsNest reserves the right to request additional documentation
- Verification may be revoked if fraudulent documents are submitted
- Verification does NOT guarantee sales or imply MomsNest endorsement of your products

---

## 4. Product Listings

### 4.1 Listing Requirements
Each product listing must include:
- Accurate **title** (max 150 characters)
- Detailed **description** of the item
- At least **1 clear image** (maximum 10 images per listing)
- Accurate **price** in the platform's currency
- Correct **category** selection
- Accurate **condition** (new, like new, good, fair)
- Current **stock** quantity
- Accurate **location** (city/region)

### 4.2 Listing Guidelines
- All images must be of the actual product (no stock photos)
- Descriptions must accurately represent the product's condition, dimensions, and features
- Prices must include all applicable costs (excluding shipping)
- Listings must not contain misleading claims or false information
- Products must comply with all safety standards for children and mothers

### 4.3 Prohibited Products
The following products may NOT be listed on MomsNest:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| **Illegal items** | Drugs, counterfeit goods, stolen property |
| **Weapons** | Firearms, knives, ammunition, explosives |
| **Unsafe products** | Recalled items, non-compliant car seats, expired consumables |
| **Adult content** | Sexually explicit items or materials |
| **Hazardous materials** | Chemicals, toxic substances, flammable materials |
| **Animals** | Live animals, animal parts |
| **Prescription items** | Prescription medications, controlled substances |
| **Tobacco/Alcohol** | Cigarettes, vaping products, alcoholic beverages |
| **Financial instruments** | Gift cards, cryptocurrency, money orders |
| **Personal information** | Data sets, user accounts, personal records |

## 5. Orders & Fulfillment

### 5.1 Order Lifecycle
```
Buyer Places Order → Seller Confirms → Seller Ships → Buyer Receives → Order Complete
     (pending)        (confirmed)       (shipped)      (delivered)       (completed)
```

### 5.2 Seller Obligations
| Obligation | Timeline |
|-----------|----------|
| **Confirm order** | Within 24 hours of placement |
| **Ship order** | Within 3 business days of confirmation |
| **Provide tracking** | At time of shipping (if applicable) |
| **Update status** | At each fulfillment stage |
| **Respond to buyer** | Within 24 hours of inquiry |

### 5.3 Shipping Responsibilities
- Sellers are responsible for packaging products safely and securely
- Sellers must ship to the address provided by the buyer
- Shipping costs and methods should be clearly communicated
- Sellers assume risk until delivery is confirmed

### 5.4 Order Cancellation
- **Seller cancellation:** Sellers may cancel orders before shipping with a valid reason. Excessive cancellations may result in account penalties.
- **Buyer cancellation:** Buyers may cancel before the order is confirmed by the seller.

## 6. Fees & Commission

### 6.1 Fee Structure
| Fee Type | Rate | When Applied |
|----------|------|-------------|
| **Listing Fee** | Free | At listing creation |
| **Transaction Commission** | 5–10% | On successful sale (deducted from payment) |
| **Promoted Listing** | Variable (coin-based) | When seller boosts a listing |
| **Withdrawal Fee** | Variable | When transferring earnings to external account |

### 6.2 Payment Terms
- Transaction revenue is credited to the seller's coin wallet
- Funds are available for withdrawal after the buyer confirms delivery or after **14 days** (whichever comes first)
- In case of disputes, funds are held until resolution
- Minimum withdrawal threshold applies

### 6.3 Fee Changes
- MomsNest will provide **30 days notice** before any fee changes
- Existing listings are not affected by fee increases until renewal

---

## 7. Disputes & Returns

### 7.1 Dispute Window
- Buyers may file a dispute within **14 days** of delivery
- Sellers must respond to disputes within **48 hours**

### 7.2 Common Dispute Reasons
| Reason | Resolution |
|--------|-----------|
| Item not received | Seller provides proof of shipping; if no proof, full refund |
| Item not as described | Seller offers return + refund or partial refund |
| Damaged item | Seller offers return + refund or replacement |
| Wrong item sent | Seller pays return shipping + sends correct item |

### 7.3 Seller Response
When a dispute is filed, sellers must:
1. Respond within 48 hours with their position
2. Provide any relevant evidence (shipping receipts, photos, messages)
3. Cooperate with MomsNest mediation if needed
4. Abide by the final resolution decision

### 7.4 Refund Responsibility
- If a refund is granted, the refund amount is deducted from the seller's wallet
- If the seller's wallet has insufficient funds, the balance will be deducted from future earnings

## 8. Seller Ratings & Reviews

### 8.1 Rating System
- Buyers can rate sellers (1–5 stars) and leave written reviews after delivery
- Seller ratings are publicly displayed on the seller profile
- Seller stats include: average rating, total reviews, response rate, total sales

### 8.2 Review Policy
- Reviews must be honest and based on actual purchase experience
- Sellers may NOT incentivize, manipulate, or suppress reviews
- Sellers may NOT retaliate against buyers who leave negative reviews
- MomsNest may remove reviews that violate Community Guidelines

## 10. Account Suspension & Termination

### 10.1 Grounds for Suspension
| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Late shipments (3+ times) | Warning → 7-day suspension |
| High cancellation rate (>10%) | Warning → listing restrictions |
| Unresolved disputes (3+ pending) | Listing suspension until resolved |
| Fraudulent activity | Immediate permanent ban |
| Selling prohibited items | Immediate permanent ban |
| Terms violation | Graduated enforcement |

### 10.2 Appeal Process
- Suspended sellers may appeal by emailing **sellers@momsnest.com**
- Appeals are reviewed within **7 business days**
- The appeal decision is final

### 10.3 Effect of Termination
- All active listings are removed
- Pending orders must still be fulfilled
- Remaining wallet balance can be withdrawn (minus pending disputes)
- Seller profile is deactivated
