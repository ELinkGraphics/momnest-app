## 1. Introduction

This document governs the terms for verified experts who contribute professional advice on the MomsNest Q&A platform. By applying for or maintaining Expert status, you agree to these terms.

## 3. Verification Process

### 3.1 Application
To apply for Expert status, submit:
1. **Professional credentials** — Degree, license, or certification documents
2. **Specialty description** — Your area of expertise and sub-specialties
3. **Years of experience** — Minimum 3 years in your field
4. **Professional bio** — Summary of your background and qualifications
5. **Contact information** — Professional email and phone number

### 3.2 Review
| Step | Timeline | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Application submitted | Day 0 | Documents uploaded through Expert Profile form |
| Initial review | 3–5 business days | Staff reviews completeness and credentials |
| Verification | 5–10 business days | Credential verification with issuing institution |
| Decision | 10–15 business days | Approved, denied, or additional info requested |

### 3.3 Verification Outcome
- **Approved:** Expert badge displayed on profile and all answers; listed in expert directory
- **Denied:** Reason provided; may re-apply after 90 days with additional documentation
- **Conditional:** Approved pending additional documentation within 30 days

### 3.4 Verification Renewal
- Expert status is reviewed **annually**
- You must confirm that your credentials remain valid
- License/certification expiration requires updated documentation
- Failure to renew results in temporary badge removal

## 4. Expert Obligations

### 4.1 Content Standards
| Obligation | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Accuracy** | Answers must be factually accurate and evidence-based |
| **Scope** | Only answer within your verified area of expertise |
| **Disclaimers** | Include appropriate disclaimers (see Section 4.2) |
| **Sources** | Cite sources for medical or scientific claims when possible |
| **Timeliness** | Information should reflect current professional standards |
| **Clarity** | Write in clear, accessible language suitable for a general audience |

### 4.2 Required Disclaimers
All expert answers must include appropriate disclaimers:

**Medical/Health Answers:**
> *"This information is for educational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for personalized medical guidance."*

**Legal Answers:**
> *"This is general legal information, not legal advice. Laws vary by jurisdiction. Consult a licensed attorney for advice specific to your situation."*

**Financial Answers:**
> *"This is general financial guidance, not personalized financial advice. Consult a qualified financial advisor for advice specific to your circumstances."*

### 4.3 Prohibited Expert Behavior
- ❌ **Diagnosing** specific medical conditions through the platform
- ❌ **Prescribing** medications or specific treatments
- ❌ **Providing** therapy or counseling sessions through Q&A answers
- ❌ **Soliciting** private consultations or clients through answers
- ❌ **Promoting** specific products, brands, or services for personal gain
- ❌ **Contradicting** established medical consensus without strong evidence
- ❌ **Answering** outside your verified area of expertise
- ❌ **Sharing** personal details about patients, clients, or cases

## 5. Professional Liability

### 5.1 Disclaimer of Liability
- MomsNest is a **platform, not a healthcare/legal/financial provider**
- Expert verification confirms claimed credentials only; it does NOT constitute a professional endorsement
- MomsNest is NOT liable for any advice given by experts on the platform
- Experts are personally responsible for the accuracy and appropriateness of their contributions
- The expert badge indicates verified credentials, not endorsement by MomsNest

### 5.2 Expert Indemnification
Experts agree to indemnify MomsNest from claims arising from:
- Inaccurate, misleading, or harmful advice provided on the platform
- Professional malpractice allegations related to platform contributions
- Violation of professional ethics codes through platform activity

### 5.3 Professional Insurance
- Experts are strongly encouraged to maintain professional liability insurance
- MomsNest does NOT provide professional liability coverage for experts

## 8. Revocation of Expert Status

| Reason | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Credentials expired and not renewed | Badge removed until renewed |
| Answering outside expertise area (repeated) | Warning → badge suspension |
| Providing harmful or dangerous advice | Immediate badge removal |
| Soliciting clients through the platform | Warning → badge removal |
| Violation of professional ethics | Investigation → badge removal |
| User complaints (pattern of poor advice) | Review → potential removal |
| Falsified credentials discovered | Immediate permanent removal + account ban |

### Appeal Process
- Experts may appeal revocation by emailing **experts@momsnest.com**
- Appeals are reviewed within **14 business days**
- Falsification-based revocations cannot be appealed
